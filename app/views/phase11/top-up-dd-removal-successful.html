Top up account

You can top up your account by using a credit or debit card, or set up a repeat direct debit.
Payment via BACS is not supported.

Credit or debit card

Use a credit or debit card to top up your pre-funded account.
If you are unable to top up online, you can call us on 0300 1239000.

Direct debit

Set up a direct debit to make regular payments to your PFA.

Cheque or Postal Order

Send a cheque or Postal Order to top up your pre-funded account to: 

Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency (DVSA),
Finance,
Ellipse,
Padley Road
Swansea,
SA1 8AN

Make sure you include your pre-funded account number, and make these payable to Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency (DVSA), crossed “Account Payee only.”
